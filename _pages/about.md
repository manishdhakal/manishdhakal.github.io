<b>CV/NLP</b>

Manish Dhakal is a Ph.D. student at Georgia State University, supervised by Dr. Yi Ding and Dr. Raj Sunderraman. He works as a Graduate Research Assistant (GRA) in the computer science department. His primary line of research is within <b>model compression</b> and <b>efficient learning</b> of computer vision (CV) and natural language processing (NLP) applications. His publications have been presented at premier venues, such as WACV, MICCAI, ACCV, IJCNLP-AACL, etc.
